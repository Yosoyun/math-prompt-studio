Start here

You only need 4 simple steps

If you can send a WhatsApp message, you can do this. No app to install, nothing to pay.

1

Pick a prompt & tap Copy

Scroll to the Prompt Library, find what you need (a solution, a question paper, a worksheet…) and press the Copy prompt button. The full instruction is now copied for you.

2

Open a free AI chat

Open ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude or Copilot in your phone or computer browser. (Links are in the AI tools box below.) Log in once - it is free.

3

Attach a photo (only if asked), then paste

If the prompt has a Photo needed tag, first click the + / image icon and add a clear photo of the question. Then paste the prompt (long-press → Paste, or Ctrl/Cmd + V).

4

Press Enter & get your result

Fill any [BRACKETS] with your details, hit send, and download or screenshot the result. Your name appears on every page automatically. Print it, share it, done.

Tip for the 5-page art styles: some AI tools make one image at a time. If so, just reply “now generate Page 2”, then Page 3, and so on - the prompt already tells the AI how to keep the same look across all 5 pages.

5 tips to get the best results

  • One question at a time. Crop the photo so only the question you want is visible.
  • Good light, flat page. A clear, straight photo helps the AI read the maths correctly.
  • Fill the [BRACKETS]. Replace things like [CLASS/GRADE] and [BOARD] with your details before sending.
  • Ask for changes. Not happy? Reply “make it simpler”, “add one more method” or “make the writing bigger”.
  • Always check the answer. AI is brilliant but not perfect - give every solution a quick glance before class.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is this really free? What is the catch?

It is completely free. There is no catch and nothing to buy here. You will need a free account on an AI tool like ChatGPT or Gemini (they also have free plans). This studio is a gift to fellow teachers from Indrajeet Yadav - the only request is to keep his signature on the outputs you share.

Do I need to know coding or be “techy”?

Not at all. If you can copy a message and paste it, you can use every single prompt here. Follow the 4 steps and you are done.

Why does “Indrajeet Yadav” appear on the results?

Each prompt politely asks the AI to sign the output - a handwritten signature on image pages, or a small “Prepared by Indrajeet Yadav” line on papers and worksheets. It is the author's way of sharing this freely while keeping his name with his work. You are welcome to use everything in your classes and coaching.

Can I use these for my school or coaching centre?

Yes - that is exactly what they are for. Make solutions, papers, DPPs and worksheets for your students as much as you like. Please keep the signature line in place when you share outputs.

The AI made a small mistake. What do I do?

AI is powerful but not flawless. Always glance over a solution before using it. If something is off, just reply “re-check step 3” or “the answer should be …” and it will correct itself. The prompts already instruct the AI to verify its work twice.

It only made one image, not five. Is it broken?

No. Some tools generate one image per message. Simply reply “now generate Page 2”, then Page 3, and so on. The prompt tells the AI to keep the same paper and handwriting style across all pages.

Which language does it work in?

English by default, but you can add “write everything in Hindi” (or any language) to the end of any prompt and the AI will follow.
