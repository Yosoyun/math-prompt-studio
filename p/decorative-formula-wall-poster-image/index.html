Decorative Formula Wall Poster (Image)

A handwritten-style decorative poster of key formulas for the classroom wall.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT or Gemini (image)

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. 1. Open an AI that can generate images.
  2. 2. Paste the prompt and fill the brackets.
  3. 3. Send and wait for the poster.
  4. 4. Proofread every formula in the image.
  5. 5. Download and print large.
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If text is blurry or wrong, reply: "Some formulas are unreadable or incorrect. Recreate the poster with each formula written clearly and correctly, larger font."
THE PROMPT
ROLE: Act as a mathematics teacher and an artist creating a decorative classroom wall poster.

CONTEXT: I teach [CLASS/GRADE] for the [BOARD or EXAM e.g. CBSE / ICSE / State Board / JEE / NEET]. I want a beautiful DECORATIVE POSTER of the most important formulas of [CHAPTER NAME] to display on the classroom wall and inspire students. This output must be an IMAGE.

YOUR TASK:
1. Design a single elegant portrait poster featuring the chapter's key formulas.
2. Use a tasteful, handwritten-chalkboard or notebook aesthetic that looks warm and inviting.
3. Arrange formulas with breathing room, a clear title, and gentle decorative touches (borders, soft accents) that do not crowd the maths.
4. Keep every formula large and perfectly legible - this is for a wall, viewed from a distance.
5. Limit to the 10 to 15 most important formulas so it stays clean.

INPUT: Here is the chapter and class. Chapter: [PASTE HERE]. Class/Board: [PASTE HERE]. (Optional: paste the exact formulas to feature - if so, use only those.)

HOW TO WORK IT OUT: First internally decide the correct shortlist of formulas and confirm each is standard and correct. When rendering, write each formula exactly and legibly; never let the decorative style distort symbols, powers, or signs. VERIFY before finalising: re-check each rendered formula is mathematically correct and clearly readable from a distance. Never invent formulas. Prefer fewer, perfectly legible formulas over a crowded page.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
1. Produce ONE portrait poster image.
2. A clear decorative title: '[Chapter] - Key Formulas'.
3. The 10 to 15 featured formulas, beautifully arranged and legible.
4. After the image, list in plain text every formula you placed, so I can proofread.

RULES: Beauty must not reduce legibility or accuracy. No fake or filler formulas. One page only.

SIGNATURE (mandatory): Sign the bottom-right of every page with an elegant handwritten signature "Indrajeet Yadav" with a small underline flourish.
