Da Vinci Inventor Codex Solution Poster

A weathered Renaissance notebook page where the maths solution looks like a hand-inked Leonardo invention study.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT Plus (image) or Gemini

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. Paste the full problem text or attach a sharp, well-lit photo of it
  2. Run the prompt and let the AI solve and verify before drawing
  3. Choose the highest-quality or HD image option if offered
  4. Zoom in and check every number, symbol and word against the original
  5. Ask for one more render if any digit or label looks off
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If the maths is wrong reply: 'Recompute the solution step by step, verify by an independent check, then redraw with the corrected numbers only.' If cluttered reply: 'Remove sketches over the text, widen margins, enlarge the steps and final-answer box.'
THE PROMPT
Create a premium vertical educational mathematics poster in the Da Vinci Inventor Codex style, approximately 4:5 portrait.

INPUT: Read the maths problem I give you (pasted here or attached as a photo). SOLVE it yourself and verify the arithmetic by an independent check BEFORE rendering. Then put the VERIFIED solution into the poster. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

FORMAT & COMPOSITION: Render the whole page as an aged Renaissance codex leaf - warm sepia and tea-stained parchment with soft foxing, faint vertical fold, deckled torn edges, candlelit amber glow with gentle vignette. Around the OUTER margins and corners only, add delicate brown iron-gall-ink engineering doodles: tiny gears, pulleys, compass-and-divider arcs, a flying-machine wing, swirling water-vortex studies, and short lines of faux mirror-writing - NEVER over the mathematics. Portrait ~4:5, generous margins, calm balanced negative space. The mathematics sits on a clean parchment column down the centre, fully unobstructed.

TYPOGRAPHY: Use elegant antique brown-ink lettering with a fine-nib quill feel for headings and a clean readable serif for the maths so every character stays legible. Render mathematical notation accurately - fractions, factorials n!, permutations nPr (or superscript P), combinations nCr or binomial coefficients, summation, subscripts and superscripts - and never distort numbers, symbols or punctuation.

CONTENT TO DISPLAY (strict top-to-bottom hierarchy): (1) TITLE '[Q-number] - [short topic title]' as an ornate inked banner; (2) a 'Problem:' block with the question VERBATIM; (3) a 'Method: [precise method name]' heading; (4) the solution as 3-6 concise steps - and where the problem calls for it, a clean central SLOT/POSITION diagram (e.g. F _ _ R with little braces labelled 'fixed') OR a small neat ledger/rank TABLE drawn as a hand-ruled codex grid; (5) a clearly boxed 'Final Answer: [value]' framed like an inked cartouche.

SIGNATURE: a small elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in a bottom corner, tasteful, not overlapping any mathematics.

ACCURACY SAFEGUARDS: copy every word and symbol exactly; verify the arithmetic independently before rendering; never drop restrictions like 'without repetition', 'distinct', 'identical', 'fixed', or 'do not come together'; show ONE method only (no alternative method); use correct permutation/combination/factorial notation; keep it readable at mobile-phone size; maintain title -> problem -> method -> reasoning -> answer hierarchy; NO malformed numerals, NO duplicated or garbled digits, NO spelling mistakes, NO pseudo-handwriting gibberish in the maths, NO random extra equations, NO decoration over the text.
