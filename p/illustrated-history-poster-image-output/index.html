Illustrated History Poster (image output)

A ready-to-print poster telling a maths story visually.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT or Gemini (image)

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. 1. Open an image-capable AI (ChatGPT or Gemini).
  2. 2. Type your topic in [TOPIC OR PERSON].
  3. 3. Paste the prompt and send.
  4. 4. Check every word in the image is spelled right.
  5. 5. Download and print for the wall.
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If text in the image is misspelled or garbled, reply: "Regenerate the poster and spell every label exactly as written; keep words short and clear."
THE PROMPT
ROLE: Act as a graphic designer creating an educational history-of-maths classroom poster.

CONTEXT: This poster is for my [CLASS/GRADE] classroom wall ([BOARD or EXAM e.g. CBSE / ICSE / State Board / JEE / NEET]). It should tell a maths story at a glance and make students curious. It will be printed on A4 or A3.

YOUR TASK:
1. Design a single vertical poster about the topic or person I name.
2. Include a bold title, 4 to 6 short milestone captions arranged as a clear visual journey (timeline, path, or panels), and small friendly illustrations for each.
3. Add one big 'WOW' fact in a highlighted box.
4. Use a clean, readable layout with strong contrast and generous spacing.
5. Keep ALL text very short so it stays legible and correctly spelled in the image.

INPUT: Here is the topic or person: [TOPIC OR PERSON]. (Optional colour theme: [COLOUR], else use bright educational colours.)

HOW TO WORK IT OUT: First privately plan the layout and confirm the few facts and dates you are sure of - do NOT put any invented date, name, or quote on the poster; if unsure, omit it or write 'approx'. Double-check every word you place is spelled correctly and is short enough to render cleanly. Because rendered text can break, prefer few words, large font, and simple labels. Keep any maths symbols minimal and clear.

OUTPUT FORMAT (the generated IMAGE must contain, in this order top to bottom):
- A bold TITLE at the top.
- A short one-line subtitle.
- 4 to 6 milestone panels or a path, each with a tiny illustration and a 3-to-6 word caption.
- One highlighted 'WOW FACT' box.
- A small footer strip.
Also, in your TEXT reply beside the image, list every caption and fact in plain text so I can verify spelling and accuracy.

RULES: Accurate, uncluttered, high-contrast, child-friendly. No fabricated facts on the poster. Short words only. Plain ASCII in your text reply.

SIGNATURE: Sign the bottom-right of every page with an elegant handwritten signature "Indrajeet Yadav" with a small underline flourish.
