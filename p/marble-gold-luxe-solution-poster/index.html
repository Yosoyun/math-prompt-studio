Marble & Gold Luxe Solution Poster

A white-marble luxury poster with veined stone, gold-leaf accents and elegant serif maths.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT Plus (image) or Gemini

  1. Paste the full prompt and add your problem text, or attach a clear, well-lit photo.
  2. Tell it to solve and double-check the arithmetic before rendering.
  3. Choose the highest quality / largest image option available.

THE PROMPT
Create a premium vertical educational mathematics poster in a Marble & Gold Luxe style, approximately 4:5 portrait.

INPUT: Read the maths problem I give you (pasted here or attached as a photo). SOLVE it yourself and verify the arithmetic by an independent check BEFORE rendering. Then put the VERIFIED solution into the poster. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

FORMAT & COMPOSITION: Render the poster as a slab of luxurious polished white Carrara marble - soft cream-white stone with delicate, realistic grey and faint gold veining drifting through it, gentle studio lighting and a subtle polished sheen. Frame the page with a refined thin gold-leaf border with ornate Art-Deco filigree corners. In the margins and corners ONLY, add tasteful brushed-gold flourishes - a slim laurel sprig, a fine geometric Deco fan, a small gilded sunburst - that catch the light. Keep a clean marble panel in the center for the mathematics. Portrait ~4:5, generous margins and abundant elegant negative space; all ornament stays in margins and corners and NEVER touches the mathematics.

TYPOGRAPHY: Use an elegant high-contrast serif (a Didone such as Bodoni/Didot feel) for headings, rendered as if engraved in shimmering gold leaf, with body text and steps in a refined dark-charcoal or warm-gold serif - all fully legible. Render every mathematical symbol accurately: fractions as proper stacked numerator/denominator, factorials as n!, permutations as nPr (or superscript-P), combinations as nCr (or binomial), summation sigma, correct subscripts and superscripts. Never distort, duplicate, or garble any digit, symbol, or punctuation.

CONTENT TO DISPLAY (strict top-to-bottom hierarchy): (1) TITLE at top: '[Q-number] - [short topic title]' in engraved gold serif capitals with a slim gold rule beneath. (2) A 'Problem:' block reproducing the question VERBATIM inside a softly inset marble panel edged in thin gold. (3) A 'Method: [precise method name]' heading in gold. (4) The solution as 3 to 6 concise numbered steps; where the problem calls for it, draw a clean central SLOT/POSITION diagram (for example F _ _ R with little braces labelled 'fixed') OR a small neat rank/ledger TABLE with thin gold gridlines on marble. (5) A clearly boxed 'Final Answer: [value]' inside an elegant gold-framed cartouche.

SIGNATURE: a small, elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in fine gold ink in a bottom corner, tasteful, never overlapping any mathematics.

ACCURACY SAFEGUARDS: copy every word and symbol exactly; verify the arithmetic by an independent method before rendering; never drop restrictions such as 'without repetition', 'distinct', 'identical', 'fixed', or 'do not come together'; show ONE method only, no alternative method; use correct permutation/combination/factorial notation; keep everything readable at mobile-phone size; maintain the title -> problem -> method -> reasoning -> answer hierarchy; produce NO malformed numerals, NO duplicated or garbled digits, NO spelling mistakes, NO pseudo-handwriting gibberish, NO random extra equations, and NO ornament over the text.
