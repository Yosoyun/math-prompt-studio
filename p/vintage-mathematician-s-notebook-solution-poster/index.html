Vintage Mathematician's Notebook Solution Poster

A vintage navy-ink research-notebook sheet for derivations and telescoping, with margin insights.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT Plus (image) or Gemini

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. Open ChatGPT (Plus, image) or Gemini.
  2. Paste your problem or attach a clear photo of it.
  3. Paste this whole prompt and send.
  4. Let it solve, verify, then render the poster.
  5. Check the numbers; reply 'regenerate, larger cleaner text' if needed.
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If the maths is wrong, reply: 'Recompute step by step, verify the answer independently, then regenerate the poster.' If it looks cluttered, reply: 'Keep all decoration in the margins only, enlarge the text, and keep the centre purely the solution.'
THE PROMPT
Create the poster from the maths problem I provide. FIRST read the problem (pasted below or attached as a photo), SOLVE it correctly and verify the arithmetic by an independent check, then fill every [INSERT ...] slot with the verified content and render ONE image. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

Create a sophisticated vertical mathematics solution sheet resembling a vintage mathematician's handwritten research notebook.

AESTHETIC
- Portrait orientation, approximately 4:5 ratio.
- Warm cream paper with subtle ageing, faint fibre texture, and slightly darker edges.
- Dark navy-blue fountain-pen handwriting for mathematics and primary text.
- Muted brown-red ink for dividers, cancellations, annotations, and emphasis.
- Sparse pencil-like marginal notes.
- The atmosphere should feel intellectual, archival, elegant, and rigorous.
- Avoid excessive decoration; the mathematical derivation is the visual focus.

TITLE
At the top centre, write in elegant cursive:
"[QUESTION NUMBER] - [CONCEPTUAL TITLE]"
Underline the title with a slightly imperfect hand-drawn line. Add one tiny star or mathematical sparkle symbol beside it.

LEFT MARGIN GOAL
Along the left margin, write a compact goal statement:
"Goal:
[INSERT THE MAIN OBJECTIVE OF THE PROBLEM]"
Example structure: "Evaluate [EXPRESSION] by spotting a hidden pattern."

PROBLEM BOX
Create a large clean rectangular box near the top. Inside it write:
"Problem:"
Then display the exact problem:
"[INSERT COMPLETE PROBLEM VERBATIM]"
Use professionally rendered mathematical notation for summation signs, factorials, limits, superscripts, subscripts, fractions, brackets, and equations.

METHOD
Below the problem box, write:
"Method: [INSERT METHOD NAME]"
Add a concise explanatory sentence:
"[INSERT THE CENTRAL STRATEGIC IDEA]"

KEY IDENTITY OR CLAIM
Write: "Claim (Key Identity):"
Place the central identity inside a prominent double-line rectangular box:
"[INSERT KEY IDENTITY EXACTLY]"

VERIFICATION
Write: "Verification:"
Show a short line-by-line algebraic verification:
[LEFT-HAND OR RIGHT-HAND EXPRESSION]
= [FIRST TRANSFORMATION]
= [SECOND TRANSFORMATION]
= [TARGET EXPRESSION]  (tick)
Use aligned equals signs. Do not skip algebraically necessary steps. Add a thin muted red dashed divider beneath this section.

MAIN DERIVATION
Use a heading such as: "[INSERT DERIVATION HEADING, E.G. 'Telescoping the Sum']"
Show:
[ORIGINAL EXPRESSION]
= [SUBSTITUTION USING THE KEY IDENTITY]
= [EXPANDED FIRST FEW TERMS]
= [VISIBLE CANCELLATION PATTERN]
= [SIMPLIFIED RESULT]
Use thin muted-red diagonal cancellation marks only over terms that genuinely cancel.
Place the resulting general formula inside a blue rectangular box:
"[INSERT GENERAL RESULT]"

FINISH
Below another dashed divider, write: "Finish:"
Then display the final substitution and solving steps:
"Given [INSERT GIVEN EQUATION], we have"
[GENERAL RESULT] = [GIVEN VALUE]
[REARRANGEMENT]
[CANCELLATION OR COMPARISON]
"Therefore, [VARIABLE] = [FINAL ANSWER]."
Place the final result inside a small double-line box at the bottom-right:
"[VARIABLE] = [FINAL ANSWER]"

MARGINAL INSIGHT
Add one small taped-paper note near the upper-right:
"[INSERT A SHORT STRATEGIC REMINDER]"
Example: "Look for a form A_k - A_(k-1) to make the sum collapse."
Add one brief handwritten reflection near the lower-left:
"[INSERT A SHORT MATHEMATICAL INSIGHT, MAXIMUM 12 WORDS]"

ACCURACY REQUIREMENTS
- All identities must be algebraically valid.
- Never invent a telescoping identity.
- Verify the endpoint terms carefully.
- Ensure cancellation marks correspond exactly to matching terms.
- Render factorials and summation limits correctly.
- Keep all text legible and avoid pseudo-handwriting gibberish.
- No bright colours, no cartoon decorations, no modern interface, and no unnecessary solution branches.

SIGNATURE: add a small, elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in a bottom corner, tasteful and not overlapping any mathematics.
