Botanical Watercolour Solution Poster

A calm botanical-watercolour poster on ivory paper with floral corners and a purple answer box.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT Plus (image) or Gemini

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. Open ChatGPT (Plus, image) or Gemini.
  2. Paste your problem or attach a clear photo of it.
  3. Paste this whole prompt and send.
  4. Let it solve, verify, then render the poster.
  5. Check the numbers; reply 'regenerate, larger cleaner text' if needed.
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If the maths is wrong, reply: 'Recompute step by step, verify the answer independently, then regenerate the poster.' If it looks cluttered, reply: 'Keep all decoration in the margins only, enlarge the text, and keep the centre purely the solution.'
THE PROMPT
Create the poster from the maths problem I provide. FIRST read the problem (pasted below or attached as a photo), SOLVE it correctly and verify the arithmetic by an independent check, then fill every [INSERT ...] slot with the verified content and render ONE image. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

Create a premium vertical educational mathematics poster in an elegant botanical watercolor style.

FORMAT AND COMPOSITION
- Portrait orientation, approximately 4:5 ratio.
- Warm ivory handmade-paper background with very subtle paper texture.
- Thin muted-olive rectangular border near the edges.
- Generous margins and balanced negative space.
- Decorative watercolor flowers and leaves only around the corners and outer margins.
- Use lavender sprigs near the upper-left corner, delicate green branches near the top-right, a soft pink peony near the lower-left, and a white lily near the lower-right.
- Decorations must never overlap mathematical text.
- The page should feel refined, calm, premium, and academically trustworthy.

TYPOGRAPHY
- Use highly legible handwritten-style lettering.
- Main title: large dark sage-green hand-lettered text.
- Section headings: purple or muted green.
- Main explanation: dark charcoal ink.
- Important variables, restrictions, and final numerical answer: selectively highlighted in purple and green.
- Mathematical symbols, factorials, fractions, combinations, and permutations must be typeset accurately.
- Do not distort letters, numbers, subscripts, superscripts, or punctuation.

CONTENT TO DISPLAY EXACTLY

TITLE:
"[QUESTION NUMBER] - [SHORT TOPIC TITLE]"

PROBLEM SECTION:
Add a small purple flower icon beside the heading:
"Problem:"
Inside a pale blush-pink watercolor rectangle, write:
"[INSERT THE COMPLETE MATHEMATICS QUESTION VERBATIM]"

METHOD SECTION:
Use a small leaf icon beside:
"Method: [INSERT METHOD NAME]"
Then show the solution as 3-5 concise bullet points.

Suggested structure:
- "[FIRST KEY OBSERVATION OR RESTRICTION]"
- "Therefore, the required pattern is:"

Create a clean central diagram showing:
[INSERT SLOT/POSITION DIAGRAM, FOR EXAMPLE: F  _  _  R]
Label fixed positions using small purple braces and the word "fixed."

Continue:
- "[EXPLAIN WHAT FILLS THE REMAINING POSITIONS]"
- "Number of ways = [FORMULA]"
- "= [SIMPLIFIED CALCULATION]"
- "= [FINAL NUMBER]"

FINAL ANSWER
At the bottom centre, create a refined double-line rectangular answer box decorated with tiny leaves.
Inside it write:
"Final Answer:"
and below it, in large purple numerals:
"[FINAL ANSWER]"

ACCURACY REQUIREMENTS
- Copy every word and mathematical expression exactly as supplied.
- Verify the arithmetic independently before rendering.
- Do not add an alternative method.
- Do not omit restrictions such as "without repetition," "distinct," "fixed," or "identical."
- Keep the solution concise enough to remain readable at mobile-screen size.
- Maintain a clear visual hierarchy: title -> problem -> method -> reasoning -> answer.
- No photorealistic objects, no modern digital interface, no excessive decoration, no spelling mistakes, and no illegible handwriting.

SIGNATURE: add a small, elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in a bottom corner, tasteful and not overlapping any mathematics.
