Stained-Glass Cathedral Light Solution Poster

A luminous stained-glass cathedral-window poster with leaded glass panels and glowing jewel colours.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT Plus (image) or Gemini

  1. Paste the full prompt and enter your problem, or attach a clear, sharp photo.
  2. Tell it to solve and verify the arithmetic before rendering the window.
  3. Pick the highest quality / largest image option offered.

THE PROMPT
Create a premium vertical educational mathematics poster in a Stained-Glass Cathedral Light style, approximately 4:5 portrait.

INPUT: Read the maths problem I give you (pasted here or attached as a photo). SOLVE it yourself and verify the arithmetic by an independent check BEFORE rendering. Then put the VERIFIED solution into the poster. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

FORMAT & COMPOSITION: Render the poster as a glowing cathedral stained-glass window, backlit so the colours luminesce - deep jewel tones of cobalt blue, ruby red, emerald green, amethyst purple and warm amber gold, divided into segments by bold dark leaded came (black/charcoal soldered lines). Frame the top with a gothic pointed-arch tracery and ornate leaded border; let soft rays of coloured light fall across the page. CRUCIAL for legibility: render the central content area as panels of clear, very pale, lightly tinted glass with strong dark lead outlines, so the text sits on calm light glass rather than busy colour. Keep the rich jewel-colour glasswork and floral rose-window motifs to the margins, corners and the arch ONLY. Portrait ~4:5, generous margins and balanced negative space; colourful glass and tracery NEVER cross the mathematics.

TYPOGRAPHY: Headings in an elegant illuminated/blackletter-influenced but still legible serif; body and steps in a clean readable serif rendered in dark lead-line lettering on pale glass - fully legible. Render every mathematical symbol accurately: fractions as proper stacked numerator/denominator, factorials as n!, permutations as nPr (or superscript-P), combinations as nCr (or binomial), summation sigma, correct subscripts and superscripts. Never distort, duplicate, or garble any digit, symbol, or punctuation.

CONTENT TO DISPLAY (strict top-to-bottom hierarchy): (1) TITLE inside the arch: '[Q-number] - [short topic title]' in illuminated lettering. (2) A 'Problem:' block reproducing the question VERBATIM on a clear glass panel outlined in lead. (3) A 'Method: [precise method name]' heading. (4) The solution as 3 to 6 concise numbered steps, each on its own pale glass panel; where the problem calls for it, draw a clean central SLOT/POSITION diagram (for example F _ _ R with little braces labelled 'fixed') OR a small neat rank/ledger TABLE built from leaded glass cells. (5) A clearly boxed 'Final Answer: [value]' in a radiant amber-glass panel with a bold lead frame.

SIGNATURE: a small, elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in dark ink on a corner glass pane, tasteful, never overlapping any mathematics.

ACCURACY SAFEGUARDS: copy every word and symbol exactly; verify the arithmetic by an independent method before rendering; never drop restrictions such as 'without repetition', 'distinct', 'identical', 'fixed', or 'do not come together'; show ONE method only, no alternative method; use correct permutation/combination/factorial notation; keep everything readable at mobile-phone size; maintain the title -> problem -> method -> reasoning -> answer hierarchy; produce NO malformed numerals, NO duplicated or garbled digits, NO spelling mistakes, NO pseudo-handwriting gibberish, NO random extra equations, and NO coloured glass over the text.
