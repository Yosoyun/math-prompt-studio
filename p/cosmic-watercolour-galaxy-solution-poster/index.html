Cosmic Watercolour Galaxy Solution Poster

A dreamy deep-space watercolour poster where the worked maths floats in a luminous nebula.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT Plus (image) or Gemini

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. Paste the full problem where it says PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo
  2. Tell it to solve and verify before drawing
  3. Ask for the highest-quality / most detailed render
  4. Check every number against your own working
  5. If anything looks off, reply with the fix and ask for one corrected version
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If the maths is wrong reply: 'The arithmetic is incorrect - recompute step by step, verify it independently, and redraw with the corrected numbers.' If it is cluttered: 'Too busy - move all nebula art into the margins only, increase negative space, and keep the equations crisp and unobstructed.'
THE PROMPT
Create a premium vertical educational mathematics poster in a Cosmic Watercolour Galaxy style, approximately 4:5 portrait.

INPUT: Read the maths problem I give you (pasted here or attached as a photo). SOLVE it yourself and verify the arithmetic by an independent check BEFORE rendering. Then put the VERIFIED solution into the poster. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

FORMAT & COMPOSITION: A deep-space watercolour wash background - inky midnight indigo bleeding into violet, magenta, and teal nebula clouds, with soft galaxy swirls, a faint dust lane, and tiny scattered white star specks like sprayed salt on wet paper. Add a thin luminous gold hairline border with delicate constellation-dot corners. Portrait ~4:5. Keep generous margins and balanced negative space; place ALL cosmic decoration (stars, nebula tendrils, a small ringed planet, comet streaks) ONLY in the margins and corners, NEVER over the mathematics. Behind the text, calm the wash into a darker translucent panel so every symbol stays perfectly readable.

TYPOGRAPHY: Elegant luminous lettering - a refined serif or graceful display face glowing pale gold and soft starlight-white, as if lit from within. Render all mathematical notation accurately and beautifully: fractions with clean horizontal bars, factorials n!, permutations nPr (or superscript-P form), combinations nCr (or the binomial bracket), summation sigma, subscripts and superscripts at correct size. Never distort numerals, symbols, or punctuation.

CONTENT TO DISPLAY (strict top-to-bottom hierarchy): (1) TITLE '[Q-number] - [short topic title]' in the largest glowing type. (2) A 'Problem:' block containing the question VERBATIM. (3) A 'Method: [precise method name]' heading. (4) The solution as 3 to 6 concise steps; where the problem type calls for it, include a clean central SLOT/POSITION diagram (for example F _ _ R with small braces labelled 'fixed') OR a small neat rank/ledger TABLE, drawn in the same starlit-gold linework. (5) A clearly boxed 'Final Answer: [value]' inside a glowing golden frame, like a captured star.

SIGNATURE: A small, elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in a bottom corner, tasteful pale gold script, not overlapping any mathematics.

ACCURACY SAFEGUARDS: Copy every word and symbol exactly; verify the arithmetic independently before rendering; never drop restrictions like 'without repetition', 'distinct', 'identical', 'fixed', or 'do not come together'; show ONE method only (no alternative method); use correct permutation/combination/factorial notation; keep it readable at mobile-phone size; maintain the title -> problem -> method -> reasoning -> answer hierarchy; produce NO malformed numerals, NO duplicated or garbled digits, NO spelling mistakes, NO pseudo-handwriting gibberish, NO random extra equations, and NO decoration over the text. The result must look like a museum-grade astronomical art print that any teacher would be proud to share.
