Classroom Rules Poster Text and Design

A printable, attractive classroom-rules poster as an image.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT or Gemini (image)

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. 1. Open an image-capable AI (ChatGPT or Gemini).
  2. 2. Paste the prompt below.
  3. 3. Fill in your class and rule list.
  4. 4. Send and wait for the poster image.
  5. 5. Ask for colour or text tweaks, then download.
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If the words are misspelled or cut off, reply: "Some text is misspelled or cut off. Regenerate the poster with the exact words I gave, large and fully inside the page margins."
THE PROMPT
ROLE: Act as a graphic designer who creates clean, motivating classroom posters for schools.

CONTEXT: This poster is for my [CLASS/GRADE] classroom. The tone should be [friendly and encouraging / firm and clear - pick one]. It will be printed on A4/A3 paper and stuck on the wall, so text must be large and readable from a distance.

YOUR TASK:
1. Design a single-page CLASSROOM RULES poster as an image.
2. Use a bold title at the top: "Our Classroom Rules".
3. List the rules I provide as short, positive lines with a small icon beside each.
4. Leave a clear margin so nothing is cut off when printed.
5. Keep the colour scheme calm and school-appropriate.

INPUT: Here are my rules, exactly as I want them worded: [PASTE 5 TO 8 SHORT RULES, one per line]. Preferred colours: [STATE COLOURS or write "you choose"].

HOW TO WORK IT OUT: Before drawing, plan the layout internally so all rules fit on one page without crowding. Spell every word EXACTLY as I typed it - do not reword, add, or drop any rule. After generating, mentally check that each rule is fully visible and correctly spelled; if any text is unclear, redo it. Never invent extra rules.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- One portrait A4 poster image.
- Title at top, rules in a clear vertical list with icons, calm background.
- High contrast between text and background for easy reading.
- After the image, give me a short text list of the exact rules used so I can proofread.

RULES: Large legible font. No spelling errors. Keep all content inside safe print margins. Use only the rules I provided.

Sign the bottom-right of every page with an elegant handwritten signature "Indrajeet Yadav" with a small underline flourish.
