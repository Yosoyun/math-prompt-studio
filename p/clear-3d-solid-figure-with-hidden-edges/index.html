Clear 3D Solid Figure with Hidden Edges

A neat 3D drawing of a solid with dashed hidden edges and all dimensions labelled.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT or Gemini (image)

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. 1. Open an image-capable AI chat.
  2. 2. Paste the prompt below.
  3. 3. Describe the solid in the INPUT line.
  4. 4. Send and check the dashed hidden edges.
  5. 5. Download the image.
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If hidden edges look solid, reply: "Make the three back edges dashed since they are hidden, and keep the visible edges solid."
THE PROMPT
ROLE: Act as a technical illustrator who draws clean, school-style 3D solids for geometry and mensuration.

CONTEXT: I teach [CLASS/GRADE] under [BOARD or EXAM e.g. CBSE / ICSE / State Board]. I need a 3D figure students can interpret for surface area and volume problems.

YOUR TASK:
1. Draw the solid in a standard slightly-tilted 3D view (oblique or isometric) so depth is clear.
2. Draw visible edges as solid lines and hidden edges as dashed lines.
3. Label every dimension I give: length, breadth, height, radius, slant height, and so on, with units.
4. Label key vertices or faces if I name them.
5. Keep the figure plain: thin black lines, light or no shading, white background.

INPUT: Here is the solid: [DESCRIBE IT HERE, e.g. "a cuboid 8 cm by 5 cm by 3 cm" or "a cone, base radius 7 cm, height 24 cm" or "a cylinder, radius 3.5 cm, height 10 cm"].

HOW TO WORK IT OUT: Restate the solid and its given measurements in one line so I can confirm. Internally check the dimensions are geometrically sensible (for a cone, confirm slant height matches radius and height by the Pythagorean relation if I give all three; flag any contradiction). Never invent dimensions I did not provide; show unknown ones as letters. Make sure hidden edges are genuinely the ones that would be behind the solid from the chosen viewpoint.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
1. CONFIRMATION: one line restating the solid and its measurements.
2. THE FIGURE: one clean 3D image with solid visible edges, dashed hidden edges, and all labelled dimensions with units.
3. NOTE: one line flagging any inconsistency you found, or "All dimensions consistent."

RULES: Hidden edges must be dashed. Every given measurement must appear with its unit. No heavy shading or colour. Plain white background.

SIGNATURE: Sign the bottom-right of every page with an elegant handwritten signature "Indrajeet Yadav" with a small underline flourish.
