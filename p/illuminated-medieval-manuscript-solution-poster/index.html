Illuminated Medieval Manuscript Solution Poster

A gilded illuminated-manuscript page with a glowing drop-cap and gold-leaf borders framing the solution.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT Plus (image) or Gemini

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. Paste the complete problem or attach a clear photo of it
  2. Let the AI solve and independently verify before illuminating the page
  3. Pick the highest-resolution image option if available
  4. Inspect every numeral and symbol against your original problem
  5. Request a fresh render if gold flourishes cover any text
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If the maths is wrong reply: 'Re-solve carefully, double-check the answer with a second method internally, then redraw using only the corrected values.' If cluttered reply: 'Shrink the gold borders, keep flourishes in the margins, and enlarge the steps and the answer box.'
THE PROMPT
Create a premium vertical educational mathematics poster in the Illuminated Medieval Manuscript style, approximately 4:5 portrait.

INPUT: Read the maths problem I give you (pasted here or attached as a photo). SOLVE it yourself and verify the arithmetic by an independent check BEFORE rendering. Then put the VERIFIED solution into the poster. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

FORMAT & COMPOSITION: Render a luxurious illuminated vellum page - creamy aged parchment with a richly decorated border of gold-leaf filigree, scrolling acanthus-leaf vines, tiny jewel-toned flowers and a small marginal drollery, in lapis blue, vermilion red, emerald green and burnished gold. A glowing decorated drop-cap initial begins the title. Decoration lives ONLY in the borders, corners and the drop-cap - NEVER over the mathematics. Portrait ~4:5, generous inner margins, calm negative space; the worked solution sits in a clean ruled text column down the centre, fully legible.

TYPOGRAPHY: Use a refined medieval blackletter/uncial feel for the title and a CLEAN highly readable book hand for the maths so every character is crisp. Render mathematical notation accurately - fractions, factorials n!, permutations nPr (or superscript P), combinations nCr or binomial coefficients, summation, subscripts and superscripts - and never distort numbers, symbols or punctuation.

CONTENT TO DISPLAY (strict top-to-bottom hierarchy): (1) TITLE '[Q-number] - [short topic title]' with an illuminated drop-cap; (2) a 'Problem:' block with the question VERBATIM; (3) a 'Method: [precise method name]' heading in rubricated red ink; (4) the solution as 3-6 concise steps - and where the problem calls for it, a clean central SLOT/POSITION diagram (e.g. F _ _ R with little braces labelled 'fixed') OR a small neat rank/ledger TABLE drawn as a gold-ruled manuscript grid; (5) a clearly boxed 'Final Answer: [value]' framed like a gilded panel.

SIGNATURE: a small elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in a bottom corner, tasteful, not overlapping any mathematics.

ACCURACY SAFEGUARDS: copy every word and symbol exactly; verify the arithmetic independently before rendering; never drop restrictions like 'without repetition', 'distinct', 'identical', 'fixed', or 'do not come together'; show ONE method only (no alternative method); use correct permutation/combination/factorial notation; keep it readable at mobile-phone size; maintain title -> problem -> method -> reasoning -> answer hierarchy; NO malformed numerals, NO duplicated or garbled digits, NO spelling mistakes, NO pseudo-handwriting gibberish in the maths, NO random extra equations, NO decoration over the text.
