Visual Infographic Formula Page (Image)

A colourful one-page infographic poster of the chapter's formulas.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT or Gemini (image)

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. 1. Open an AI chat that can create images.
  2. 2. Paste the prompt and fill the brackets.
  3. 3. Send and wait for the poster image.
  4. 4. Check every formula in the image is correct.
  5. 5. Download and print as a poster.
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If a formula in the image is wrong or garbled, reply: "Formula [name] is incorrect or unreadable in the image. Redo the poster with the correct formula written clearly."
THE PROMPT
ROLE: Act as a mathematics teacher and a poster designer creating a classroom infographic.

CONTEXT: I teach [CLASS/GRADE] for the [BOARD or EXAM e.g. CBSE / ICSE / State Board / JEE / NEET]. I want a colourful one-page INFOGRAPHIC POSTER of the key formulas of [CHAPTER NAME], to print and pin on the classroom wall. This output must be an IMAGE.

YOUR TASK:
1. Design a single vertical A4 poster (portrait) that presents the chapter's key formulas.
2. Group formulas into clear visual blocks with small headings and gentle colour coding.
3. Use simple icons or shapes to separate sections, but keep formulas large and readable.
4. Make it attractive but not cluttered - a student should grasp the layout at a glance.
5. Use a clean, professional colour scheme suitable for a school wall.

INPUT: Here is the chapter and class. Chapter: [PASTE HERE]. Class/Board: [PASTE HERE]. (Optional: I may paste the exact list of formulas to include - if so, use only those.)

HOW TO WORK IT OUT: First, internally decide the correct list of formulas for this chapter and class, and confirm each is standard and correct. When rendering the image, write every formula exactly and legibly - do not let the design distort symbols, powers, or signs. VERIFY before finalising: re-check that each formula shown is mathematically correct and spelled right. Never invent formulas. If a symbol is hard to render, choose a clear plain-text form instead.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
1. Produce ONE portrait A4 infographic image.
2. Title at the top: '[Chapter] Formulas - Class [X] ([Board])'.
3. Include a small header block reading 'Compiled by Indrajeet Yadav'.
4. Grouped formula blocks with mini headings.
5. After the image, list in plain text every formula you placed, so I can proofread it.

RULES: Formulas must be correct and readable - readability beats decoration. No fake or extra formulas. Keep to one page.

SIGNATURE (mandatory): Sign the bottom-right of every page with an elegant handwritten signature "Indrajeet Yadav" with a small underline flourish.
