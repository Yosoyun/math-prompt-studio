Transit / Subway Map Solution Poster

A sleek metro-map poster where the solution steps become stations along a coloured transit line.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT Plus (image) or Gemini

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. Paste the full problem or attach a clear photo of it
  2. Let the AI solve and verify the answer before drawing the line
  3. Select the highest-quality image setting if offered
  4. Trace each station label and number against your original problem
  5. Ask for another render if any station text is wrong or crowded
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If the maths is wrong reply: 'Recompute and verify independently, then relabel the stations with the corrected values only.' If cluttered reply: 'Reduce the number of stations to the key steps, straighten the line, and enlarge the terminus answer node.'
THE PROMPT
Create a premium vertical educational mathematics poster in the Transit / Subway Map style, approximately 4:5 portrait.

INPUT: Read the maths problem I give you (pasted here or attached as a photo). SOLVE it yourself and verify the arithmetic by an independent check BEFORE rendering. Then put the VERIFIED solution into the poster. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

FORMAT & COMPOSITION: Design a clean modern metro-map poster - flat off-white or pale-grey background, a single bold coloured transit line (e.g. crisp cobalt, teal or coral) that snakes top-to-bottom with smooth 45-degree bends, classic round interchange-style station dots with white centres, and subtle thin grid registration marks at the edges. Decorative transit motifs - a small compass rose, a route key, a tiny train pictogram - sit ONLY in the margins/corners, NEVER over the mathematics. Portrait ~4:5, generous margins, lots of clean negative space.

TYPOGRAPHY: Use a crisp geometric sans-serif (Johnston/Helvetica transit feel) so every label is razor-sharp and legible. Render mathematical notation accurately - fractions, factorials n!, permutations nPr (or superscript P), combinations nCr or binomial coefficients, summation, subscripts and superscripts - and never distort numbers, symbols or punctuation.

CONTENT TO DISPLAY (strict top-to-bottom hierarchy): (1) TITLE '[Q-number] - [short topic title]' as a bold route headboard at the top; (2) a 'Problem:' block with the question VERBATIM in a clean info-panel; (3) a 'Method: [precise method name]' heading styled like a line name; (4) the solution as 3-6 concise steps shown as consecutive STATIONS along the line, each station a labelled stop - and where the problem calls for it, a clean central SLOT/POSITION diagram (e.g. F _ _ R with little braces labelled 'fixed') OR a small neat rank/ledger TABLE styled as a tidy timetable; (5) a clearly boxed 'Final Answer: [value]' as the bold terminus station at the bottom.

SIGNATURE: a small elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in a bottom corner, tasteful, not overlapping any mathematics.

ACCURACY SAFEGUARDS: copy every word and symbol exactly; verify the arithmetic independently before rendering; never drop restrictions like 'without repetition', 'distinct', 'identical', 'fixed', or 'do not come together'; show ONE method only (no alternative method); use correct permutation/combination/factorial notation; keep it readable at mobile-phone size; maintain title -> problem -> method -> reasoning -> answer hierarchy; NO malformed numerals, NO duplicated or garbled digits, NO spelling mistakes, NO gibberish station labels, NO random extra equations, NO decoration over the text.
