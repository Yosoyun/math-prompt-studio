Visual One-Pager Notes (Image)

A single-page visual summary image with boxes, icons and key formulas.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT or Gemini (image)

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. 1. Open an AI that can create images.
  2. 2. Paste the prompt below.
  3. 3. Fill in chapter, class and board.
  4. 4. Send and wait for the page.
  5. 5. Print on A4 for handouts.
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If words run off the page, reply: "Reduce the text, enlarge the fonts, and keep everything inside the A4 margins." If a formula is wrong, reply: "Correct that formula and redraw the whole page."
THE PROMPT
ROLE: Act as a mathematics teacher and a visual-notes designer who makes beautiful single-page chapter summaries.

CONTEXT: I teach [CLASS/GRADE] for [BOARD or EXAM e.g. CBSE / ICSE / State Board / JEE]. I want one A4 VISUAL ONE-PAGER (an actual image) that summarises a chapter using neat boxes, simple icons, colour, and the key formulas - the kind of page a student keeps for last-minute revision. This output must be a generated image.

YOUR TASK:
1. Design one portrait A4 page summarising the chapter I name.
2. Use 4 to 6 labelled boxes or zones: Key Definitions, Main Formulas, Steps to Solve, Common Mistakes, Quick Tips, and one Worked Example.
3. Add small, simple icons or accents to make zones easy to find.
4. Keep the most important formulas large and central.

INPUT: Here is the chapter and points to include: [PASTE CHAPTER AND KEY POINTS HERE]. If only the chapter name is given, use standard [BOARD] content for [CLASS/GRADE].

HOW TO WORK IT OUT: Decide all content first and VERIFY every formula and the worked example twice (syllabus accuracy plus a numeric sanity check) BEFORE drawing. Never invent formulas or values. Spell everything correctly. Keep text short and large enough to read when printed; do not overflow the margins.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- One portrait A4 image, clean and colourful, well organised into labelled zones.
- Title strip at the top: "<Chapter> - One Page Notes | Compiled by Indrajeet Yadav".
- Zones: Definitions, Formulas, Steps, Common Mistakes, Quick Tips, Worked Example.
- High contrast, no overlapping text, comfortable margins.

RULES: Accuracy and legibility above decoration. Everything must fit on one printable page. No clutter.

SIGNATURE: Sign the bottom-right of every page with an elegant handwritten signature "Indrajeet Yadav" with a small underline flourish.
