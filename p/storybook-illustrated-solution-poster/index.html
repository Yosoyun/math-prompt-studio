THE PROMPT
Create a premium vertical educational mathematics poster in the Storybook Illustrated style (a charming children's picture-book page for younger classes), approximately 4:5 portrait.

INPUT: Read the maths problem I give you (pasted here or attached as a photo). SOLVE it yourself and verify the arithmetic by an independent check BEFORE rendering. Then put the VERIFIED solution into the poster. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

FORMAT & COMPOSITION: A cosy hand-painted picture-book page with soft watercolour and gouache washes, gentle paper grain, and a warm pastel palette - buttercream, sky blue, mint, peach, soft coral. A friendly rounded border like a storybook frame: little hills, clouds, stars, a curious rabbit or owl, pencils and number blocks - all tucked in the MARGINS and CORNERS only, never over the mathematics. Portrait ~4:5, generous airy margins and plenty of calm white space so a young reader is never overwhelmed. Soft, even, daylight lighting; no harsh shadows.

TYPOGRAPHY: Rounded, friendly, hand-lettered storybook lettering that is large, cheerful, and FULLY legible (clarity comes first). Render mathematical notation accurately in clean numerals: fractions with clear horizontal bars, factorials as n!, permutations as nPr, combinations as nCr (or the binomial bracket), summation sigma, subscripts and superscripts correctly placed. Never distort numbers, symbols, or punctuation - young learners must read them perfectly.

CONTENT, strict top-to-bottom hierarchy: (1) TITLE on a cute ribbon or banner: '[Q-number] - [short topic title]'. (2) A 'Problem:' block (on a soft paper card) with the question reproduced VERBATIM. (3) A 'Method: [precise method name]' heading on a small painted tag. (4) The solution as 3 to 6 short, simple steps, each on its own gentle row; where the problem type calls for it, include a clean central SLOT/POSITION diagram (e.g. F _ _ R with little braces labelled 'fixed') drawn as friendly boxes, OR a neat rank/ledger TABLE drawn as a tidy storybook chart. (5) A clearly boxed 'Final Answer: [value]' inside a bright, happy rounded box (a star or trophy badge beside it is fine, in the margin).

SIGNATURE: a small, elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in a bottom corner, tasteful, not overlapping any mathematics.

ACCURACY SAFEGUARDS: copy every word and symbol exactly; verify the arithmetic independently before rendering; never drop restrictions like 'without repetition', 'distinct', 'identical', 'fixed', or 'do not come together'; show ONE method only (no alternative method); use correct permutation/combination/factorial notation; keep it readable at mobile-phone size; maintain title -> problem -> method -> reasoning -> answer hierarchy; NO malformed numerals, NO duplicated or garbled digits, NO spelling mistakes, NO pseudo-handwriting gibberish, NO random extra equations, NO cute decoration over the text. Deliver ONE single finished poster image.
