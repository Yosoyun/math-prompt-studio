Technical CAD Drawing Solution Poster

A precise engineering-blueprint poster with title block, grid, and crisp technical lettering around the solution.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT Plus (image) or Gemini

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. Paste the problem exactly or attach a clear photo
  2. Instruct it to solve and verify the arithmetic first
  3. Ask for the most detailed, high-resolution render
  4. Cross-check the numbers and every stated condition
  5. If it is wrong or cramped, reply with the fix and request one corrected version
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If the maths is wrong reply: 'Recompute and independently verify the answer, then redraw with the corrected figures in the title block.' If it is cluttered: 'Thin out the grid and annotations, enlarge the text, and keep all technical marks out of the equation areas.'
THE PROMPT
Create a premium vertical educational mathematics poster in a Technical CAD Drawing (engineering blueprint) style, approximately 4:5 portrait.

INPUT: Read the maths problem I give you (pasted here or attached as a photo). SOLVE it yourself and verify the arithmetic by an independent check BEFORE rendering. Then put the VERIFIED solution into the poster. Problem: [PASTE THE COMPLETE PROBLEM HERE, or attach a clear photo].

FORMAT & COMPOSITION: A precise architectural blueprint sheet - deep blueprint-blue (or a clean drafting-white) background with a faint fine engineering grid, a crisp double-line drawing border, and a professional title-block strip along the bottom edge. Add subtle drafting motifs ONLY in the margins and corners: dimension lines with arrowheads, a small north arrow, a scale bar, registration crosshairs, and a 'DRAWN BY' cell - NEVER over the mathematics. Portrait ~4:5. Keep generous margins and disciplined negative space; the working area stays clean so every symbol is razor-sharp. Lines are thin, exact, and white/cyan on blue (or charcoal on white).

TYPOGRAPHY: Uppercase technical drafting lettering - even, monospaced-feeling engineering stencil style - perfectly legible. Render all mathematical notation accurately and cleanly: fractions with exact horizontal bars, factorials n!, permutations nPr (or superscript-P), combinations nCr (or binomial bracket), summation sigma, subscripts and superscripts at correct size. Never distort numerals, symbols, or punctuation.

CONTENT TO DISPLAY (strict top-to-bottom hierarchy): (1) TITLE '[Q-number] - [short topic title]' as the drawing title at the top. (2) A 'Problem:' block containing the question VERBATIM inside a ruled callout box. (3) A 'Method: [precise method name]' heading on a labelled bar. (4) The solution as 3 to 6 concise steps drawn like numbered construction notes; where the problem type calls for it, include a clean central SLOT/POSITION diagram (for example F _ _ R with little dimension braces labelled 'fixed') OR a small neat rank/ledger TABLE rendered as a precise drafting table with ruled cells. (5) A clearly boxed 'Final Answer: [value]' placed in a bold rectangular title-block cell.

SIGNATURE: A small, elegant handwritten signature 'Indrajeet Yadav' in a bottom corner near the title block, tasteful, not overlapping any mathematics.

ACCURACY SAFEGUARDS: Copy every word and symbol exactly; verify the arithmetic independently before rendering; never drop restrictions like 'without repetition', 'distinct', 'identical', 'fixed', or 'do not come together'; show ONE method only (no alternative method); use correct permutation/combination/factorial notation; keep it readable at mobile-phone size; maintain the title -> problem -> method -> reasoning -> answer hierarchy; produce NO malformed numerals, NO duplicated or garbled digits, NO spelling mistakes, NO pseudo-handwriting gibberish, NO random extra equations, and NO decoration over the text. The result must look like an authentic, precision-drafted engineering sheet a teacher would be proud to share.
