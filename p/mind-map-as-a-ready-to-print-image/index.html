Mind Map as a Ready-to-Print Image

A colourful, printable mind-map image of one chapter on a single page.

Makes images - needs an image AI  ·  Best tool:  ChatGPT or Gemini (image)

✅ How to use this effectively

  1. 1. Open an AI that can create images.
  2. 2. Paste the prompt below.
  3. 3. Fill in chapter, class and board.
  4. 4. Send and wait for the picture.
  5. 5. Save or print the image.
🔧 If it is not right, reply with this: If text is blurry or misspelled, reply: "Redraw with larger, perfectly spelled labels and fewer words per box." If it is too crowded, reply: "Use only 5 main branches and make the layout cleaner with more white space."
THE PROMPT
ROLE: Act as a mathematics teacher and a visual designer who creates classroom mind-map posters.

CONTEXT: I teach [CLASS/GRADE] for [BOARD or EXAM e.g. CBSE / ICSE / State Board / JEE]. I want a single-page mind-map IMAGE of one chapter that I can print on A4 and stick on the wall or hand out. This output must be an actual generated image.

YOUR TASK:
1. Create one clean, colourful mind-map image of the chapter I name.
2. Put the chapter name in a central bubble.
3. Draw 5 to 6 main branches radiating outward, each a different colour, each clearly labelled.
4. Under each branch add 2 to 4 short child nodes with the key idea or formula.
5. Make it look neat, balanced and printable - not cluttered.

INPUT: Here is the chapter and the points I want covered: [PASTE CHAPTER NAME AND KEY POINTS HERE]. If I give only the chapter name, use the standard [BOARD] syllabus content.

HOW TO WORK IT OUT: First decide the full content and check every formula and term for correctness twice (once for syllabus accuracy, once with a quick sanity check) BEFORE drawing. Never invent formulas or values. Keep every label short so it stays readable in the image - prefer 2 to 5 words per node. Spell every mathematical and English word correctly. Use clear, large, high-contrast text.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- One landscape A4 mind-map image.
- Central bubble: chapter name (largest text).
- 5 to 6 coloured main branches, each labelled.
- Short child nodes hanging off each branch.
- A small clean title strip at the top reading: "<Chapter> - Mind Map | Compiled by Indrajeet Yadav".
- Plenty of white space; no overlapping lines or text.

RULES: Accuracy first - a wrong formula on a poster is worse than none. Keep it uncluttered and genuinely printable. No watermark text other than what I asked for. Use a consistent, friendly colour scheme.

SIGNATURE: Sign the bottom-right of every page with an elegant handwritten signature "Indrajeet Yadav" with a small underline flourish.
